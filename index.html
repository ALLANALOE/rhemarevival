Spiritual Leadership

Meet Apostle David Eagle Davids

15+

Years Serving

Leading With Faith and Vision

Apostle David Eangle Davids has been the spiritual shepherd of Rhema Revival Church since 2025. His dynamic preaching style combines deep biblical insight with practical life application, making complex theological concepts accessible to all.

Apostle David Eagle Davids served in various ministry roles before answering God's call to lead this congregation. He is passionate about discipleship, community transformation, and equipping believers for ministry.

"My greatest joy is seeing people discover their God-given purpose and walk in the fullness of Christ."
